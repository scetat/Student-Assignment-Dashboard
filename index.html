    14 foolproof tips for writing a great assignment.

    1. Set aside a specific time to do your homework
    2. Take a break every hour.
    3. Prioritize tasks.
    4. Create a study schedule.
    5. Make sure to complete the most pressing assignments first.
    6. Break down larger projects into manageable tasks.
    7. Don't multitask.
    8. Find a comfortable, but not too comfortable, place to work.
    9. Minimize social distractions.
    10. Minimize noise.
    11. Ask your parents or peers for help.
    12. Ask your teacher for help or clarification.
    13. Find a tutor (if available).
    14. Go to the library.
